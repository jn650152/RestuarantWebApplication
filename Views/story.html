Story

This post is referenced from online material

1795: Alexander Keith is born in Halkirk, Scotland on October 5.
1812: At the age of seventeen, Keith travels to Sunderland, England where his uncle teaches him the brewerying and malting business.
1817: Keith sets sail across the Atlantic and arrives on the shores of Nova Scotia.
1820: Alexander Keith's Nova Scotia Brewery opens its doors for the first time.
1840: In recognition of Keith's political and social involvement, Queen Victoria appoints him to the Legislative Councile of Nova Scotia. He serves this post for the next 13 years.
1843: Alxander Keith is elected Mayor of Halifax.
1853: Keith is re-elected as Mayor.
1863: keith's Brewery on Lower Water Street is producing over 100,000 litres of ale pr year. That's equivalent to about 1,700 modern kegs annually or, on average, 547 pints of beer a day.
1867:47 years after the establishment of Alexander Keith's Birthday. Canada establishes itself as a nation.
1869: An active member of the Freemasons for many years. Keith rises through the ranks and becomes the Grand Master of Nova Scotia.
1873: ALxander Keith passes away at the age of 78.
1917: Two munitions ships collide in Halifax Harbor, igniting the largerst manmade explosion in the pre-atomic era. Thousands are wounded or kiled. The brewey is spared from destruction.
1939: With a declaration of war on Germany, Halifax's population nearly doubles. Troops from all over Canada are introduced to teh taste of Alxander Keith's india Pale Ale.
1955: The Angus L. Macdonald Bridge opens, connecting Dartmouth to Halifax. And making it that much easier for Dartmouthians to get their hands on a Keith's.
1995: World leaders converge on Halifax for the G7 Summit. We can only assume they came for the Keith's.
2005: Alxander Keith's Red Amber Ale lands on shelves across Canada.
2008: Alexanfer Keith's Premium White joins the growing lineup of beers.
2012: The introdction of Alexander Keith's Original Cider.
2013: The Hop Series is introduced, consisting of Cascade Hop Ale, Hallertaur Hop Ale and The Hop Mason.
